Risk and Benefits

Benefits

  • Devices like security cameras help keep your safe or help track down any thief that would otherwise be almost impossible.
  • Technology can help people save time and energy such as electronic thermostats to quickly check the temperature in an area or the temperature of someone.
  • When used correctly technology can help people be organized and access information quickly.

Risks

  • Devices like phones or computers can collect a large amount of personal information like location, conversations, cookies, etc.
  • Many devices have weaknesses that can be exploited and allow hackers to access personal information and devices.
  • Overusing technology can lead to reduced attention span and cause more distractions.
